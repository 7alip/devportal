FAQ

What are the dates for the rsk grants program?
The grants program will commence applications on 01/12/2019, and will run until 01/12/2020
I have an idea that I think would be a good fit, but it does not fit into one of the categories mentioned above. Is that acceptable?
Absolutely! We are all ears to your suggestions, and will review them. One of the reasons we created this grants program is that we would like to hear what the development community wants and needs.
Who is eligible to apply for the grants program?
Anyone who is not an employee of rsk or IOVlabs is eligible. You should also have the ability or know how to build the solution that you have proposed.
How much can I receive for my application?
The total amount to be awarded for all applicants is $200,000 USD. The amount awarded to each individual application is determined on a case-to-case basis.
How are applications assessed?
Each project or idea submitted is assessed on a large number of factors by the rsk team. The main themes for the criteria are whether it is a good fit, and the ability to deliver.
What happens after I submit my application?
When we have reviewed your project or idea, we will start an email correspondence with you in order to get more details and provide feedback.
